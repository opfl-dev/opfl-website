OPFL researches and provides real solutions to reduce or eliminate chronic pain without medication.

Our mission is to support research and the development of treatments for chronic pain.

The solution was discovered by scientists at the University of North Carolina and was perfected at the University of Marburg in Germany. The German randomized and published clinical trial resulted in 82% of female fibromyalgia patients maintained their pain freedom at the 12-month follow-up.

As a registered non-profit in the US, all of the contributions are tax-deductible. Funds donated go directly into helping patients, replicating the German results in the US, and obtaining FDA approval.
